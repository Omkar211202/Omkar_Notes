## Step 5:Literature Review
### **Paper 1: Can ESG Indices Improve the Enterprises’ Stock Market Performance?—An Empirical Study from China**
written by: Xiang Deng and Xiang Cheng (2019)

- Research based out of China
- Built a model by using the financial indicator method.
- collected ESG scores given by 2 companies in China
- Used the DID test to prove the correlation between the stock market and ESG.
- Did a Regression analysis for the same.
- showed that there is a positive correlation bw ESG score of the company and its share value
- the developed value that will evaluate the EPS of the current year using the ESG score of this year and EPS of the last year(EPS used as a metric to evaluate stock performance)

 Backward Chaining:
-  Qiu, M.; Yin, H. An analysis of enterprises’ financing cost with ESG performance under the background of ecological civilization construction. J. Quant. Tech. Econ. 2019, 36, 108123
- Kushwaha, G.; Sharma, N. Green initiatives: A step towards sustainable development and firm’s performance in the automobile industry. J. Clean. Prod. 2016, 121, 116–129. [CrossRef]

Forward Chaining:

### **Paper 2: ESG Performance and Stock Price Volatility In Public Health**
Dongyi Zhou and Rui Zhou (2022)

- Tried to develop a linear regression model that has volatility as the dependent variable based on ESG, Size, Leverage, Cash, etc.
- did a bench mark analysis.
- Robustness test was done to show the efficiency of this test.

Backward Chaining:

### **“Paper 4: The impact of ESG inclusion on price, liquidity and financial performance of Indian stocks: Evidence from stocks listed in BSE and NSE ESG indices”**
Suresha B. ,Srinidhi V. R. , Dippy Verma , Manu K. S. , Krishna T.A (2022)

- tried to test out the impact on ESG inclusion (Nifty100 ESG index and BSE 100 ESG index) and the impact on the stock market.

## Step 6: Identifying Research Gaps:

This is the broad summary of the literature review and some gaps they did not answer:
- The above research articles provide a broader perspective in terms of geographies studied.
- The above have been dated from about 2013-17
- Different linear models have been used by different researchers, to estimate stock return and ESG.
- None of the papers have talked about sector wise ESG impact to the stocks.
- Only one research work has been published from India which talks about synchronization of ESG and markets, but does not deal with sector wise impact, and speaks about overall impact alone.

A study on the impact of change in ESG Performance and its relation to stock price shifts across different sectors in the Indian Market.
